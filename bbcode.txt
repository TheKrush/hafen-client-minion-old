Custom Haven &amp; Hearth Client

[i]This is a fork of [url=http://www.havenandhearth.com/forum/viewtopic.php?f=49&amp;t=40754]Ender's client[/url] so it'll contain [i]most[/i] of the changes made to that client.[/i]

[b]Installation:[/b]
Download [url=http://thekrush.github.io/hafen-client-minion/files/hafen-minion.jar]hafen-minion.jar[/url] (to a empty folder) and run it. It will download all necessary files to folder where it is placed.
